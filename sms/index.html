SMS Privacy Policy

What We Collect

We collect your first name, last name, and mobile phone number when you voluntarily submit them through our internal opt-in form.

How We Use It

Your phone number is used solely to send SMS reminders for professional development workshops, scheduled meetings, and board communications. We do not use your number for marketing, fundraising, or promotional purposes.

Who Sees It

Your phone number is stored in a secure internal system accessible only to authorized Learning and Development staff. We do not sell, share, or disclose your phone number to third parties.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will not receive more than 3 messages per week.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opting Out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts. You may also contact the Learning and Development team directly to have your number removed.

Questions

Contact Elizabeth Garcia, Chief Learning Officer, at garciae@childrensguild.org or 443.829.6155.

This policy was last updated April 2026.
